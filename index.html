Who We Are


Dr. Zimmer is a comprehensive general dentist preforming a broad variety
of procedures, including cosmetics, endodontics, implants, and oral medicine.

We provide the most evolved form of dentistry available in a pleasant and personal setting. Our office is a
quirky combination of teeth, technology, art and humor surrounded by a rooftop garden in the center of the city.

Dr. Zimmer is a traditional dental provider who is one of a few in Seattle that is Board Certified in both Dentistry and Implantology. We offer a variety of preventative and restorative services. In the event that a specialist is required, Dr. Zimmer has strong working relationships with professionals in the Medical-Dental Building. Our twenty eight years in this location attests to our reliability. As lives get busier and time more valuable, we work hard to serve our patients in an efficient and caring manner.

We accept most insurances, but it's the patients' responsibility to contact member services to make
sure we are a preferred provider, or if they have any out-of-network coverage. Dental insurance is intended
to cover some if not all of your dental care, depending on your plan. As a courtesy, we will gladly forward
your charges onto your insurance company. Most plans include deductibles, maximums, and coinsurance,
which are ultimately the patient's responsibility. If you have dental insurance, please bring your plan information
with you and we will make sure that you receive the maximum benefits your policy provides. We always advise
our patients to call their insurance company and get familiar with the coverage.

Please contact us with any questions. We're here to help you!
